Tesjor Launch

March 30, 2017

From the Royal University of Phnom Penh’s CJCC, Pathmazing launches Cambodia’s first true eCommerce platform to initially focus on the food and beverage sector, and later transportation, hotels, activities and tour guides. Our name “Tesjor” is derived from the Khmer word “tourism.”

For our launch we focused on the F&B sector. We have signed 85 restaurant outlets and more than 50 outlets have been fully trained and ready to start accepting orders today. Some of these restaurants include Domino’s Pizza, KFC, Lotteria, Tous Les Jours and Cafe Amazon.

The Tesjor platform includes an app for customers, an app for the restaurant operator and various web admin pages. It’s a true eCommerce platform because it provides an end-to-end digital solution for the entire ordering, payment and delivery process. Only e-payment is accepted and cash on delivery (COD) is not an option.

Since Tesjor requires e-payment, we have established strong relationships with all the major local payment providers. At launch the available e-payment options are Tesjor Wallet, Wing Money, ACLEDA, PayGo kiosks and PayWay that includes Visa and Mastercard. Our most important strategic partner is Wing, who will be managing our Tesjor Wallet, which is an e-wallet where users can top-up money to be used later to quickly make purchases in Tesjor and other eCommerce apps. With the Tesjor Wallet, it’s tied to the Tesjor account. To make any purchase, users simply need to enter their Tesjor password.

Working together with epayment providers, restaurants, hotels, tour guides, tour operators and the government, we’re well positioned to digitally transform Cambodia’s tourism and hospitality industry into the best in the region.
